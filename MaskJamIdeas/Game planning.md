## Theme - The Night Shift
Psychological body horror - identity crisis
- VHS filter and audio quality
## Gameplay
- 2011 image jump scares
	- Flashes into the character's past
- Lots of creepy sounds in the background
- First-person
	- The player and everyone is wearing a mask
		- Everyone looks at you weird if you take off the mask
	- What do the masks do?
		- Get stronger with mask
		- Mask of different ranks change the person - got to level up the masks
			- Everyone is against me

- **The player is in an office**
	- Build-up party to New Years'
		- We have a week to build up to this
		- We find out that the end that the player is in fact insane
	- They are doing basic tasks
	- There is a chance to help people but you don't know if you are helping
	- Change the lighting - atmospheric horror
		- White, dim lighting
		- Yellow, dim lighting
	- Increase the whites of the eyes and light reflects from eyes too brightly

## Mask relation
- Player is more resistant to mask's madness than others
	- The madness oscillates up and down
	- Everyone else is straight up going mad and you are left questioning this
- Only the sane notices the insane things happening inside the game
